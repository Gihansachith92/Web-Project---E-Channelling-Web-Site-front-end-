Ambulence

Helth Care has developed a name over the years for its reliable ambulance service, as well as for offering convenient home care visits and a trusted sample collection service. The trust we have built among the public in providing these auxiliary medical services reinforces our reputation for offering greater access to quality affordable healthcare for all Sri Lankans. We operate a modern, comfortable and fully-equipped ambulance fleet that functions 24/7 year-round Medical Helth Care, offered at affordable prices.
